Our Team

OUR TEAM

Jaiwanth Mane

CEO and Co-Founder

Jaiwanth worked for a couple of EduTech companies like Talkative Minds and Stumagz, even while completing his Bachelor’s in Electronics and Communicatios from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. Being a curious mind in this tech-savvy world, naturally made him dig deeper into the problems that are stopping him and the other students from reaching their dreams. Sensing that there’s a legitimate and desperate need for new practices in the educational ecosystem, he co-founded Hamzek Solutions with his long-time friend Anurag Palla.

Anurag Palla

Co-Founder

Anurag returned to India in 2017 after his Master’s (in Industrial Engineering) at Purdue University, to add value to his family’s business and his country. Hailing from a family of renowned educationists and having studied in the world-class institutions like PSG College of Technology and Purdue, Anurag has a clear and distinct idea of what our today’s education ecosystem is and a vision, to see what it can be. As a natural risk-taker and with zeal to make a difference, Anurag started his stint as an angel investor believing in his friend’s vision and thus co-founded Hamzek Solutions.
